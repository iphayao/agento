# SoClean — Channel Strategy

Five active channels with distinct content formats, audience behavior, and performance metrics.

---

## Channel Overview

| Channel | Primary goal | Primary persona | Content type |
|---|---|---|---|
| TikTok Shop | Awareness → conversion | มิ้น (Gen Y) | Entertainment + demo |
| Shopee | Conversion + repeat | มิ้น + คุณแม่บ้าน | Product listing + review |
| Lazada | Conversion + B2B | พี่โอ๋ (Office) + Reseller | Listing + bulk pricing |
| Facebook | Awareness + community | คุณแม่บ้าน + all | Social posts + ads |
| Reseller / LINE | B2B acquisition | เจ้ของร้าน | Direct + relationship |

---

## TikTok Shop

### Role in the Funnel
Top-of-funnel discovery and bottom-of-funnel conversion for impulse buyers.

### Content Format
- **Video length:** 15–60 seconds optimal for organic; 6–15 seconds for ads.
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical.
- **Caption:** 50–100 Thai characters + hashtags.
- **Hook:** First 2 seconds must grab attention. Use movement, text overlay, or surprise.

### Content Types That Work
1. **Demo video:** Open a box, show texture, show low-dust behavior.
2. **Review reaction:** Real reaction to first use.
3. **Use case:** "ทิชชู่ SoClean ที่โต๊ะทำงาน" or "เช้าๆ ที่บ้าน"
4. **Value callout:** "900 แผ่นในราคาแค่นี้?"
5. **TikTok Live:** Q&A + unboxing + limited-time offer.

### Voice on TikTok
- Conversational, energetic, native Thai language.
- Trending sound usage is encouraged.
- Avoid overly polished corporate look — authenticity wins here.
- Text overlays in Thai, large and readable on mobile.

### Posting Frequency
- Organic: 3–5 videos per week.
- Live sessions: 1–2 per week during peak hours (19:00–22:00).

### KPIs
- Views, engagement rate, click-to-shop rate, conversion rate.

## Shopee

### Role in the Funnel
Primary conversion channel. Main storefront for household buyers.

### Product Listing Structure

**Title format:**
```
[Brand] [Product type] [Key specs] [Key benefit]
SoClean ทิชชู่หน้า 2ชั้น 180แผ่น แพ็ค5กล่อง ฝุ่นน้อย เนียนนุ่ม
```
- Max 100 characters.
- Include key search terms: ทิชชู่, ทิชชู่หน้า, กระดาษทิชชู่.
- Avoid prohibited claims in title.

**Description structure:**
1. Hook headline (20 words Thai)
2. Product specs table (2-ply, 180 sheets, pack of 5)
3. Key benefits (3–4 bullet points, claim-safe)
4. Use cases (home, office, family)
5. Shipping and handling notes
6. Review highlights (3–5 selected reviews)

**Description length:** 500–1,000 characters.

### Images
- Main image: product on clean white/light background.
- Gallery image 2: open box showing tissue.
- Gallery image 3: lifestyle — person using at desk or home.
- Gallery image 4: value/pack size callout graphic.
- Gallery image 5: key benefits infographic (claim-safe).

### Pricing Strategy
- Individual pack pricing prominently.
- Bundle pricing (2 packs, 3 packs) with discount shown.
- Carton pricing inquiry note for resellers.

### Review Management
- Respond to all reviews within 24 hours.
- Use 5-star reviews as social proof in marketing content.
- Address negative reviews constructively; never argue.

## Lazada

### Role in the Funnel
Office and reseller acquisition; brand credibility.

### Differences from Shopee
- More office and B2B buyer traffic.
- Product titles: 120 character limit.
- Business buyers care about: invoice availability, bulk pricing, reliability.

### Product Listing Additions
- Mention: "สำหรับออฟฟิศ ซื้อยกลังได้"
- Add carton pricing option if platform supports.
- Add business contact / wholesale inquiry in description.

### Description Focus
- Lead with office use case: "ฝุ่นน้อย เหมาะสำหรับสำนักงาน"
- Include: 180 sheets × 5 boxes = 900 sheets per pack.
- Include: carton = 50 packs = 45,000 sheets.

## Facebook

### Role in the Funnel
Brand awareness, community building, retargeting ad platform.

### Organic Content
- **Post frequency:** 3–5 per week.
- **Format mix:** Image posts (50%), video (30%), text/poll (20%).
- **Caption length:** 100–250 characters Thai + image.
- **Hashtags:** 3–5 per post (less than TikTok).

### Content Types for Facebook
1. **Product lifestyle posts:** Natural photos of tissue in home/office.
2. **Value posts:** Pack size, carton pricing, math graphics.
3. **Review reposts:** Customer review screenshot + thank you reply.
4. **Reseller call-to-action:** "ร้านค้าสนใจซื้อยกลัง ทักมาเลย"
5. **Seasonal / event content:** Thai holidays, back-to-school, etc.

### Facebook Ads
- **Objective:** Conversions (to Shopee/Lazada/TikTok Shop).
- **Targeting:** Women 25–45, Thailand, interest in home goods, family.
- **Ad format:** Video (15–30s) or carousel image.
- **Copy:** Short, direct, claim-safe. Use approved phrases only.

### Tone on Facebook
- Slightly warmer and more community-oriented than TikTok.
- Longer captions acceptable for storytelling posts.
- Engage with comments. Build community, not just broadcast.

## Reseller Channel (LINE / Direct)

### Role in the Funnel
B2B acquisition and retention of resellers buying by carton.

### Communication Format
- LINE Official Account for broadcast to reseller list.
- Direct messaging for individual reseller onboarding.
- Monthly carton promotions via LINE broadcast.

### Content for Resellers
1. **Carton pricing sheet** (updated monthly).
2. **Product photos** for resellers to use in their own listings.
3. **Review / testimonial** showing customer satisfaction.
4. **Reorder reminder** when average reorder cycle approaches.

### Key Messages for Resellers
- "สินค้ามีรีวิวดี ขายง่าย"
- "ส่งเร็ว รับได้เร็ว สต็อกได้ทันที"
- "ลูกค้าซื้อซ้ำ กำไรสม่ำเสมอ"
- "ยกลัง 50 แพ็ค ราคาส่ง"

### Tone on Reseller Channel
- Businesslike but friendly — relationship-building tone.
- Be direct about pricing and delivery terms.
- Respond quickly; resellers value responsiveness.

## Cross-Channel Content Repurposing

Content production is expensive. Repurpose smartly:

| Source | Repurpose as |
|---|---|
| TikTok video | Facebook Reel + YouTube Short |
| Customer Shopee review | Quote card for TikTok + Facebook |
| Product photo | Shopee gallery + Lazada gallery + LINE image |
| Carton value graphic | Reseller LINE broadcast + Facebook ad |
| TikTok demo video | Shopee product video embed |

## Channel Claim Safety Reminder

All channels must follow `approved-claims.md` and `prohibited-claims.md`. Platform moderators (Shopee, Lazada) may flag absolute claims. TikTok's automated systems also flag health and safety-adjacent content. Claim-safe language reduces moderation risk across all channels.
