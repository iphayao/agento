All claims listed here are **safe to use in marketing copy** without requiring additional proof documentation. They are grounded in product specifications, manufacturing intent, or verifiable customer experience.

## Softness Claims (Safe with Appropriate Phrasing)

| Approved phrasing (Thai) | Approved phrasing (English) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| เนียนนุ่ม | Soft and smooth | Core differentiator; use freely |
| สัมผัสนุ่ม | Soft to the touch | Sensory claim; safe |
| ให้สัมผัสที่นุ่มสบาย | Comfortable, soft feel | Descriptive; safe |
| นุ่มเหมาะสำหรับใบหน้า | Soft, suitable for face use | Safe without medical claim |
| ลูกค้าบอกว่านุ่ม | Customers say it's soft | Social-proof attribution |

**Do not use:** นุ่มที่สุด / softest / นุ่มกว่าทุกยี่ห้อ (superlatives require proof)

## Low-Dust Claims (Safe with Appropriate Phrasing)

| Approved phrasing (Thai) | Approved phrasing (English) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| ฝุ่นน้อย | Low-dust | Core claim; safe |
| ฝุ่นน้อยกว่า | Reduced dust | Comparative; acceptable without named competitor |
| ให้สัมผัสสะอาด | Gives a clean feel | Sensory framing; safe |
| ไม่ระคายเคืองจากฝุ่น (ลูกค้าบอก) | Less dust irritation (as reported by customers) | Social-proof framing; safe |
| ฝุ่นน้อย เหมาะกับการใช้ในออฟฟิศ | Low-dust, suitable for office use | Contextual claim; safe |

**Do not use:** ไร้ฝุ่น 100% / dust-free / zero dust / ไม่มีฝุ่น (absolute claims; require verified lab data)

## Social Proof Claims (Safe When Attributed)

| Approved phrasing (Thai) | Notes |
|---|---|

| รีวิวดีจากลูกค้าจริง | Use only if review data supports it |

## Claim Approval Checklist

Before publishing any claim, confirm:

- [ ] Claim is in this approved list, OR
- [ ] Claim is a direct product specification fact, OR
- [ ] Claim is attributed to verified customer review data
- [ ] Claim does not use any word from `prohibited-claims.md`
- [ ] Superlatives (ที่สุด, best, most) are not used unless proven
