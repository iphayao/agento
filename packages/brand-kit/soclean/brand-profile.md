# SoClean — Brand Profile

## Brand Identity

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Brand name | SoClean |
| Company | BN Paper |
| Category | Facial tissue |
| Country of market | Thailand |
| Primary language | Thai (with English for select channels) |

## Positioning Statement

**Thai:** "สะอาด เนียนนุ่ม ฝุ่นน้อย"

**English equivalent:** "Clean feel, soft touch, low-dust tissue for everyday life."

SoClean is positioned as the **everyday practical tissue** for Thai households, offices, and resellers who want soft, low-dust tissue at honest value — not a premium luxury, not a commodity throwaway.

| Specification | Detail |
|---|---|
| Type | Facial tissue |
| Ply | 2-ply |
| Sheets per box | 180 sheets |
| Pack size | Pack of 5 boxes |
| Carton size | 50 packs per carton |
| Target use | Face, hands, desk, home, office |
| Key differentiator | Low-dust / reduced-dust |
| Texture | Soft, smooth |

## Brand Promise

SoClean gives you a **clean, smooth tissue experience with less dust** — suitable for daily use at home, office, and shop.

We do not claim to be the absolute softest, the dustless, or medically certified. We claim to be a **genuinely good daily-use tissue at real value**.

## Brand Values

1. **Honest** — We say what the product actually does, not what sounds impressive.
2. **Practical** — Designed for real daily life, not aspirational marketing.
3. **Accessible** — Good quality that households and small shops can afford.
4. **Consistent** — Reliable experience pack after pack.

## Competitive Frame

- Better than generic commodity tissue on softness and dust reduction.
- Positioned below medical-grade or premium imported tissue.
- Strong on carton value for resellers and office buyers.

## Visual Identity Notes

- Primary color palette: clean whites, soft blues/greens (cleanliness cues).
- Photography: natural light, everyday home/office settings.
- Avoid clinical sterile imagery (no lab coats, no test tubes).
- Prefer warm, familiar, relatable people and spaces.

## Brand Voice Summary

Friendly, honest, warm, practical. Speaks Thai naturally. Avoids jargon. Never over-promises.
