# SoClean — Customer Personas

Four primary buyer personas, ordered by volume and marketing priority.

---

## Persona 1: น้องมิ้น — The Smart Gen Y Household Buyer

**Priority:** Primary / Highest volume

### Profile
- **Age:** 27–35
- **Gender:** Female
- **Location:** Bangkok suburbs, provincial cities
- **Occupation:** Office worker, teacher, small business owner
- **Income:** 20,000–45,000 THB/month
- **Household:** Rents or owns condo/house; 2–4 family members

### Shopping Behavior
- Shops on Shopee and Lazada weekly.
- Follows TikTok creators for product discovery.
- Reads reviews before buying any FMCG product.
- Buys household staples in bundle packs to save on shipping.
- Shares good finds in LINE groups with family.

### What She Wants from a Tissue
- Soft enough to use on her face without irritation.
- Not dusty — she hates the powder residue on dark surfaces.
- Good value per pack.
- Reliable delivery from a seller with good reviews.

### Pain Points
- Tissues that leave white specks on dark clothing.
- Boxes that are hard to open.
- Products that don't match the listing description.
- Sellers with slow shipping.

### Key Message for Her
> "ทิชชู่ที่นุ่มสบาย ฝุ่นน้อย สั่งได้ทุกเดือน"

### Content That Resonates
- Before/after comparison showing dust difference.
- Honest review-style TikTok video.
- "ลองใช้จริง" (real use test) content.
- Bundle value highlight: "900 แผ่นในแพ็คเดียว"

## Persona 2: คุณแม่บ้าน — The Practical Family Manager

**Priority:** High / Large purchase volume

### Profile
- **Age:** 35–50
- **Gender:** Female (primary household purchaser)
- **Location:** Any region; often semi-urban or provincial
- **Occupation:** Homemaker, or employed with family responsibilities
- **Household:** 3–6 family members including children and elderly

### Shopping Behavior
- Shops primarily on Lazada and Shopee.
- Prefers sellers with high ratings and return policies.
- Buys by the pack or carton to reduce re-ordering frequency.
- Price per unit matters more than brand story.
- Influenced by family members' recommendations.

### What She Wants
- Soft enough for children's faces.
- Low dust — especially important with elderly family members.
- Reliable, predictable quality pack after pack.
- Good price for a large-family household.

### Pain Points
- Inconsistent quality between purchases.
- Tissue that irritates her children's skin.
- Boxes that are hard for elderly to access.

### Key Message for Her
> "ทิชชู่ดี ทนทาน ใช้ได้ทั้งบ้าน ราคาไม่หนักกระเป๋า"

### Content That Resonates
- Value math: "1 แพ็ค = 900 แผ่น ใช้ได้กี่วัน?"
- Family use scenario: kitchen, bathroom, kids' desk.
- Customer review from similar household buyer.

## Persona 3: พี่โอ๋ — The Office Supply Manager

**Priority:** Medium / High AOV per order

### Profile
- **Age:** 28–42
- **Gender:** Male or female
- **Role:** Office manager, admin, procurement coordinator
- **Company size:** SME (5–100 staff)
- **Budget responsibility:** Monthly office supplies budget

### Shopping Behavior
- Buys in bulk; prefers carton pricing.
- Evaluates cost per unit and reliability of supply.
- Prefers sellers who can issue receipts or invoices.
- Shopee Business or Lazada for Business preferred.
- Repeat purchase if quality and delivery meet expectations.

### What She/He Wants
- Consistent quality — no colleague complaints.
- Low dust — important in office environments (screens, keyboards).
- Easy to reorder with the same seller.
- Reasonable carton price.

### Pain Points
- Tissue dust settling on keyboards and monitors.
- Running out of stock and having to emergency reorder.
- Colleagues complaining about rough or low-quality tissue.

### Key Message for Him/Her
> "ทิชชู่ฝุ่นน้อย เหมาะออฟฟิศ ซื้อยกลังคุ้มกว่า"

### Content That Resonates
- Office use case: "ฝุ่นน้อย ไม่รบกวนหน้าจอและคีย์บอร์ด"
- Carton pricing highlight.
- "สต็อกได้ ไม่ขาดมือ" messaging.
- Invoice/receipt availability note.

## Persona 4: เจ้ของร้าน — The Reseller / Shop Owner

**Priority:** Strategic / Highest single-order value

### Profile
- **Age:** 30–55
- **Gender:** Any
- **Business type:** Convenience store, online reseller, mini-mart, market stall, Facebook Live seller
- **Location:** Any region; often provincial
- **Buying unit:** Carton (50 packs)

### Shopping Behavior
- Buys by carton or multi-carton.
- Evaluates: margin, sell-through speed, return/complaint rate.
- Compares wholesale pricing before committing.
- Builds relationship with suppliers who deliver reliably.
- May reorder 1–4× per month depending on volume.

### What They Want
- A product their customers like and re-buy.
- Good margin at carton pricing.
- Low complaint / return rate.
- Fast and reliable delivery.
- A brand name that has some recognition or social proof.

### Pain Points
- Customers complaining about tissue quality.
- Supplier delivery delays causing stockouts.
- Products that are hard to sell (no reviews, no recognition).

### Key Message for Them
> "ทิชชู่ที่ลูกค้าซื้อซ้ำ ส่งเร็ว กำไรดี ซื้อยกลังได้เลย"

### Content That Resonates
- Reseller testimonials.
- Carton volume and price transparency.
- "สินค้ามีรีวิวดี ขายง่าย" angle.
- Clear product photos suitable for resale listings.
- Fast shipping and wholesale inquiry process.
